What Can You Do?
  1. Volunteer: These are welcoming communities that will enrich volunteers personally. Volunteering is also a great opportunity to network with a wide cross-section of tech professionals.
  2. Guest Presenter: Add to our content with one time or multiple presentations on new, exciting or advanced computer science or STEM related topics.
  3. Financial Support: All funds go directly to programs and there are no administrative costs.
  4. Hardware Donations: Laptops and other computers to be refurbished and distributed to students.
  5. Partner On New Courses: If you have any ideas or things you'd like to offer, we'd be happy to collaborate.
  6. Mentor: Sign up to meet individually with graduates to support their continued learning.
  7. Internships: these students can be a valuable resource for both startups and established companies.
  8. Employment: Hire refugees.
